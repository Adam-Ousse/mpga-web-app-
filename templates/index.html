About the Project

MPGA uses artificial intelligence to democratize exoplanet discovery.
See the NASA Space Apps Challenge: "A World Away: Hunting for Exoplanets with AI"

Challenge summary: Data from several space-based exoplanet missions have enabled discovery of thousands of new planets, but most were identified manually. With advances in AI/ML, it's now possible to automatically analyze these large datasets to find exoplanets. The challenge: create an AI/ML model trained on NASA's open-source exoplanet datasets to accurately identify new exoplanets.
